What this is

Everyone else is shouting
from inside the chaos.

Half the world is screaming that AI will save everything; the other half, that it will end everything. Both are shouting from inside the storm wall, where the wind is loudest and nothing can be seen clearly.

A windstorm has one place where the air is still: the eye. That's where this is written. Every week The Windstorm takes what actually happened — not the hype, not the doom, the signal — and explains what it means for the people whose work and lives are being rearranged by it. Plainly. Calmly. With one concrete thing to do about it.

Read it as a five-minute letter, or watch it as the show. Same storm, same eye — your choice of window.
